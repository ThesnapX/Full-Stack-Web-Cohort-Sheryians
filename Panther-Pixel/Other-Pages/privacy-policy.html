Privacy Policy for Panther Pixel

Panther Pixel we respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our SEO and YouTube thumbnail design services. By engaging with our services, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Information We Collect

We only collect the minimal data necessary to deliver our services. This includes your name, email address, and temporary access to your YouTube channel (strictly for uploading/publishing thumbnails or optimizing SEO). We do not store login credentials, payment details, or any other sensitive information.

How We Use Your Data

Your name and email are used solely for communication, such as project updates, invoices, and customer support. YouTube channel access is temporary and revoked immediately after project completion. We do not retain access beyond the delivery of your thumbnails or SEO work.

Data Storage & Security

All collected data (name and email) is stored securely in password-protected Google Sheets, accessible only to our core team. We do not use cookies, tracking tools, or analytics on our website.

Data Retention & Deletiony

Once your project is complete, we permanently remove all YouTube channel permissions and delete any unnecessary records. The only data we may retain (with your permission) includes:

Final deliverables (e.g., thumbnail files, SEO reports).

Testimonials or reviews you voluntarily provide.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with advertisers, marketers, or external agencies. Your information is strictly used in-house to deliver the services you’ve requested.

Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your stored data (name/email) at any time by contacting us at [Your Email Address]. Note that we cannot remove project deliverables (e.g., thumbnails we’ve designed for you) as they are part of our service records.

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted here, and your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
